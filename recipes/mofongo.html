Mofongo

The mofongo it's a Puertorican cusine which is based on fried the smash green platains. The origin of the plate is unkown however it's quite popular around the island of Puerto Rico.

The ingredients needed are the following:

  • Plantains
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Butter

It's quite simple to cook:

  1. Peel the Platainns.
  2. Choped them into small pieces.
  3. Deep fried them.
  4. Get some garlic, salt and butter.
  5. Use a "pilon" which is like puertorican version of a mortar to smash the platain mixed with the step 4 ingredients.
  6. Finally removed the mofongo and enjoyed it with the sauce and protein of choice.
