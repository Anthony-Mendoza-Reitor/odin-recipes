Pina-Colada

The Pina-Colada it's a Puertorican cocktail which gain popularity over the years as one of the top choices when it comes to tropical beverages/cocktails.

The ingredients needed are the following:

  • Pineapples
  • Coconut Cream
  • White Rum ~DONQ or Bacardi~
  • Ice
  • Whipped Cream
  • Cherry

It's quite simple to cook:

  1. Add a can of coconut cream to a blender.
  2. Add a big can of pineapple.
  3. A hand full of ice.
  4. Desired amount of Rum.
  5. Blend all for 3 minutes.
  6. Serve with whipcream.
  7. Final touch add a cherry on top.
